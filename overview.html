GroceryVision aims to bring together computer vision and AI scientists from both academic and industry backgrounds, focusing on pioneering advancements in physical retail AI, particularly within the grocery sector. This collaboration encompasses several key components, including: hosting publicly available datasets developed by entities such as Amazon AWS and other sponsors, formulating and refining problems aimed at addressing real-world issues, organizing workshop challenges to showcase developments and exchange learnings, and encouraging participation from the community in this initiative.
